about

TaskTemple helps you achieve zen with your to do list, by allowing you to organise your list of things to do into projects.

We try to keep it simple, and so have focused on providing core features such as:

  • collapsible task notes
  • project (and task) priority
  • project progress bar based on number of tasks completed
TaskTemple also provides persistent storage of your todo list. Projects and tasks will be saved automatically to your browser's local storage, meaning they will still be available even after exiting the window.

To achieve ultimate todo list zen, you can also login using your google account and access your to do list from any device!
